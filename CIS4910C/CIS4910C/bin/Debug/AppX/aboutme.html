Background:

As an adult, loans and paying bills becomes a regular part of your daily life and can take over quickly. Learning the most efficient way to take care of those bills and paying off your loans is one of the toughest things to do. Some people are fortunate with high paying jobs but for most of us, money doesn't come that easily.

Purpose

To help friends and family, acquaintances and strangers, with paying off their loan debt. To give them resources on how they can pay off their loans faster and save more money.
